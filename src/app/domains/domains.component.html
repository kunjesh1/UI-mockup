Auto Email Response and Email Routing
Try It
Problem
  • For a large volume of emails, manual query resolution to 1st email is a tedius task.
  • Handling escalation emails is a tedious and manual process as it gets assigned to queues
  • Identifying Email attributes manually is tricky and complex


Solution
  • Automated AI-based system that can suggest appropriate responses to the agents.
  • Automated responses can be integrated with 1 click integration with existing systems like Zendesk or can be independently used as api endpoints.
  • Identification of email context, topic, skillset,product department,line of business etc which can be used for email routing to the right agent.


Requirements from Client
  • Min.of 1000 email and 1st replies to be uploaded onto the platform for training Email Auto Response
  • Min of 1000 email and their corresponding attributes or department routed to be uploaded for training Email Routing.
