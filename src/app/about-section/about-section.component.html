Our mission is to automate the enterprise AI use case
Wordsense is an NLP and speech engineering and research company focussed on building Automl solutions and custom apis for enterprises. At wordsense, we want to help enterprises operationalise AI. We truly believe that AI technology should be more accessible and cheap.
Here is state of play today

Most big players either solve these problems with a big internal team of ML researcher, Platform engineers, full stack developer and business analysts or outsource to some technology company with similar team size.

Usually it take couple to months to deploy only a POC grade solution which is not scalable by design for production. That takes another couple of months.

Average cost for a POC grade solution is in the range of $100k to $200k for 8-12 weeks engagement. Also considerable amount of time in invested in collecting data and tagging it

Usually no deep expertise in NLP/NLU/Speech in these solutions since it requires years of similar problem solving or deep research oriented teams. Hence the solution is often discarded or the production pipeline drastically changes.

Machine learning models keep advancing and given the nature of solution,solutions provided by a service focussed player fails to upgrage or perform good over time. Also there is a huge investment involved in support or any enhancement engagement

Here is how we can do better

We believe that if we ship, we should own it. Our platform constantly upgrades the underlying technology with state of the art research. Which means, we don't buy customer's time or money to upgrade the apis and offering.

Most clients don't have a lot of usable data to start off. Our platform by design, uses less amount of data to jump start or fine-tune the models powering our existing apis.

We also believe that the best AI doesn't come from boxed solution offered by a team. We thing we can provide powerful apis that developers can use on top of their work for solutioning.

Analytics dashboard for all the platform offerings with customizable KPI cards. This gives quick insights to anything that goes live.
